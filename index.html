Common Essay Prompts & Examples

üíº "Tell us about yourself and your goals"

Tips: Focus on your passions, experiences, and how this scholarship helps you achieve specific goals. Be authentic and specific.

‚ùå Weak Example:

"I am a good student who works hard. I want to go to university to study business because it will help me get a good job. This scholarship will help me pay for school."

Why it's weak: Too generic, no specific details, doesn't show personality or clear goals.

‚úÖ Strong Example:

"Growing up in my family's small retail shop, I witnessed firsthand how entrepreneurship transforms communities. Helping my parents manage inventory and customer relationships sparked my passion for business analytics. At Singapore Polytechnic, I'm pursuing Business Studies with a focus on data-driven decision making. This scholarship would enable me to complete my degree and launch a social enterprise helping local SMEs digitize their operations."

Why it's strong: Personal story, specific examples, clear connection between past and future, shows impact.
